Eligibility Criteria
  • Completion of Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) or its equivalent examination with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as compulsory subjects..
  • Candidates must clear the entrance examination conducted by the university/board for the respective courses.
  • Candidates usually need to appear for and qualify in entrance examinations like the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) or national-level exams such as JEE Main or other state-level engineering entrance exams.
  • Specific courses may have additional eligibility criteria or prerequisites, communicated during the admission process.
  • Applicants from international education boards need to provide equivalent qualification certificates as per university guidelines.
  • Reserved categories may have different eligibility requirements, as per government regulations.
  • The candidate must have secured a minimum aggregate mark in these subjects(Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry), often around 50% to 60%, depending on the college and category (general, SC/ST, etc.).

Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and accessible to all aspiring students. Follow the steps below to secure your place:

  1. Fill out the online application form by clicking the button below.
  2. Submit the required documents, including academic transcripts and standardized test scores.
  3. Appear for the entrance examination conducted by the university/board for the respective courses.
  4. Attend the counseling session, where you'll have the opportunity to discuss your course preferences and clarify any queries.
  5. Upon successful completion of the above steps, you will receive an admission offer.
  6. Pay the admission fees to confirm your enrollment.
